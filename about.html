About Me

My name is Kirsten and I'm from New York City. I'm a senior at Cornell University studying Electrical Engineering. I also have minors in Classics and History, with a focus on Roman literature and history. In the Fall of 2021, I will be returning to Cornell to pursue a Master's Degree in Biomedical Engineering. I have a very diverse skill set in both engineering and the humnaities. At Cornell, I play with the Women's Club Soccer Team, of which I am the captain and former president. My leadership skills extend to my community involvement. I am a volunteer firefighter with the Cayuga Heights Fire Department, where I am involved with training new members. I am interested in medical devices, bio-inspired robotics, and creating technology that makes a difference.

If you are looking to learn more about my experiences or would like to collaborate on future projects, please reach out to me via email. I can be reached at kes334@cornell.edu.
