Wizard's Chess

We designed and programmed an voice-controlled chess board. Players speak their move into a computer, and their pieces are automatically moved across the board. We use python to directly communicate with an arduino and move magnetic chess pieces via servos. Our game allows the visually or physically impaired to move chess pieces entirely through voice command.

Used: Ardiuno, Python

FRDM Board Pedometer

Here we turn the FRDM board microcontroller into a real-time handheld pedometerr. We utilize the on-board accelerometer to allow the user to count steps over an arbitrary or pre-set interval. Additionally, the board can be calibrated to accomodate different gaits for accurate tracking.

Used: FRDM Board, C, I2C
